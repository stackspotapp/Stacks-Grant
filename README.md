# StacksPot Grant Milestones & Progress

This repository tracks the development progress, milestones, and deliverables for **StacksPot**, a decentralized pot-based participation platform built on the **Stacks blockchain**.

StacksPot introduces a gamified model for participating in STX-powered pools, where users can contribute to on-chain pots and engage in transparent, community-driven reward cycles. The project aims to increase ecosystem engagement by making participation in stacking-inspired mechanisms more interactive and accessible.

## Key Areas of Development

### Pot Creation & Platform Activity
Continuous deployment of new pots to maintain active participation cycles and increase user engagement on the platform.

### New Pot Mechanics

Development and integration of new pot types including:

- **StackSpot Crowd Funding Pots** – community-driven funding pot type:
A **Crowd Funding Pot** allows communities or individuals to contribute **STX** into a shared pot that is delegated for stacking. The generated yield is used to fund a specific project or goal defined by the pot creator. At the end of the pot cycle, **contributors receive back their principal**, while the **yield is sent to the designated project or goal address**.

- **StackSpot Sequential Pots** – structured reward distribution across rounds pot type:
A **Sequential Pot** is a cooperative stacking pot where participants delegate **equal STX amounts** into a pot. The **number of participants determines the number of cycles**, and yield is distributed **sequentially based on join order**, with each cycle paying the next participant. After all cycles are completed, **all participants receive back their original principal**.

### Platform Development

Ongoing improvements to smart contracts, frontend user experience, and platform stability.

### Community Growth

Participation campaigns, bounty programs, and ecosystem outreach designed to expand StacksPot adoption.

### Security & Stability

Code reviews, security improvements, and best practices to ensure safe and reliable platform operations.
